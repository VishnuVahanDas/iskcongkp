ISKCON Life Membership

The ISKCON Life Membership program was introduced in early 1970’s as an opportunity to become an integral part of the ISKCON family by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupad. He invited everyone across the world to take advantage of residing in ISKCON temples all over the world for a couple of days, attend spiritual programs and receive Krishna conscious association. Presently more than 1 million life patrons are part of ISKCON’s family.

Benefits to life patrons

As a Life Patron member, you will be entitled to receive the following benefits:

  • You receive great opportunities to serve Lord Krishna and thus express your gratitude to Him.
  • You will receive a centrally issued hologram card with your photo and patron number along with an updated comprehensive list of ISKCON centers around the world.
  • A set of books by His Divine Grace A.C Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada. Available in English or Hindi
  • Free Invitations to weekly programs in homes around the world.
  • Japa Mala and beadbag to chant Hare Krishna mahamatra
  • Your donation is tax exempt under section 80-G of the income tax act, 1961. (India) .
  • Your donation will help propagate Krishna consciousness all over the globe.
  • You donation will be used for construction of Karnataka’s grand Jagannath Mandir.
  • Wherever guest house facilities are available, with prior confirmation, you will get 3 days of free stay and prasadam once a year.
